========================================================================
                     CITYPARK PERMIT SYSTEM
========================================================================

WHAT IS THIS PROJECT ABOUT?
---------------------------
CityPark is a comprehensive, modern web application designed to digitize 
and streamline the entire lifecycle of city parking permits and enforcement.
It replaces manual, paper-based parking management with a secure, 
cloud-connected platform. The system handles everything from vehicle 
enrollment and document verification to fine payments and field citations.

WHO CAN USE IT?
---------------
The platform is built around a Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system 
tailored for three specific types of users:

1. Residents (Citizens):
   Everyday users who need to park in city zones. They can use the self-
   service portal to enroll their vehicles, apply for different types of 
   permits (Residential, Guest, Employee), upload proof of residency, 
   pay fees securely, and dispute any citations they receive.

2. City Admins (Staff/Clerks):
   City government employees who oversee the parking program. They have 
   access to an administrative dashboard where they can review incoming 
   permit applications, verify uploaded documents, approve or reject 
   permits, and handle citation disputes. They also get a bird's-eye 
   view of system KPIs and revenue.

3. Enforcement Officers (Police/Traffic Wardens):
   Field workers who patrol parking zones. Using a mobile-optimized 
   tool, they can instantly look up a vehicle's license plate to verify 
   its permit status in real-time. If a vehicle is parked illegally, 
   they can seamlessly issue a citation and calculate the fine.

HOW CAN IT BE HELPFUL?
----------------------
- Convenience for Citizens: No more waiting in lines at city hall. 
  Residents can apply for permits and pay fines 24/7 from their phones.
- Operational Efficiency: Automates the approval pipeline for admins, 
  reducing paperwork and administrative overhead.
- Real-Time Enforcement: Officers get instant, color-coded feedback on 
  permit validity, ensuring accurate ticketing and fewer false citations.
- Data-Driven Decisions: The system provides detailed analytics on 
  citation hotspots and revenue collection, helping cities optimize.
- Transparency: Clear tracking of applications, payments, and disputes 
  builds trust between the city and its residents.
